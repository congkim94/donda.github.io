About Us

If you live in Cincinnati or the surrounding area in OH, then you have picked the perfect site to find a veterinarian for your cat! Dr. Feline & Dr. Garfield are licensed veterinarians, treating only cats. Your cat's health and well being is very important to us and we will take every step to give your cat the best possible care.
