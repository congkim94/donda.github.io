DONDA

A Content, Experience & Product Company founded by Kanye West

ABOUT

DONDA is a creative content company founded by American creative Kanye West. It was announced via Twitter by West. According to West, DONDA is a creative company with "amazing thinkers", where they can "bounce their dreams and ideas" with the "goal to make products and experiences that people want and can afford". DONDA has released multiple projects in different categories such as film, album arts, music videos, print, and stage designs. In Feb 2016, West posted an image of DONDAs organizational chart on Twitter. The chart contains a network of businesses including everything from alternative energy sources, to marketing, to hotels, and to medical reserch in cancer and HIV.
